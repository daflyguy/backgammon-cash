How to Play Backgammon

Backgammon is a classic board game that requires strategy and skill. Here are the basic rules:

  • Each player starts with 15 checkers on the board.
  • The objective is to move all your checkers around the board and bear them off before your opponent does.
  • Players roll two dice and move their checkers according to the numbers rolled.
  • Checkers can only move to open points (points not occupied by two or more opposing checkers).
